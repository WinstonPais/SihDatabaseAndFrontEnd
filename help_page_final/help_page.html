      • Hiring a lawyer

      • - How many lawyers can I send a request to at a time?

        You can send a request to only one lawyer at a time. If he/she rejects your request then, you will get a notification and then, you can send a request to another lawyer.

      • - How do I know if the selected lawyer is genuine?

        After registration, the lawyers will be verified by checking with the Bar Council of India. Additional documents may be asked to be uploaded for verification. This is to ensure that all lawyers are genuine.
